Visited Portofino Overviews Entertainment
Coming to Portofino, visitors will find themselves lost in classic color films, even experiencing the sociable lifestyle of the rich. Enjoy celebrity fun in the quaint Portofino village, world-renowned as the first resort destination. The village is a celebrity favorite, but still has a sparkling feel at the top of the coastline, right in front of the curving hill. Visitors can walk on the promenade and see the colorful houses on the coast. Please browse the chic boutiques, sip on regional wines and enjoy the freshest seafood and olives in the bar area with a stage as you watch the action unfold around the cove. Portofino is still a pretty but well-known village since it became a popular vacation spot in the 19th century. The village was a must-see on the itinerary of the rich in the 50s and 60s and still remains. has not lost its own charm in the process of developing into a modern resort town. The restaurants with small stages here do not know how many tourists come to this gem in the Ligure Sea. The small coastal square called "Piazzetta" overlooks the boats bobbing in the waves while anchored in the bay.
